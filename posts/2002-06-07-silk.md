title: Silk
---
[Unsanity](http://www.unsanity.com) has released [Silk](http://www.unsanity.com/haxies/silk/), a preference pane for Mac OS X that enabled Quartz text rendering in Carbon applications. Requires Mac OS X 10.1.5, available on [Apple's site](http://www.info.apple.com/support/downloads.html) or in SoftwareUpdate.
