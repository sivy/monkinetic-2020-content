Protip - Macbook Pro 16" connected to a Samsung 32" 4K UHD monitor (I got mine at Costco) may benefit from using the Displayport, not HDMI. HDMI connection, at least through a USB-C to HTML adapter, was too laggy. Got a USB-C to DisplayPort cable and it's no longer lagging.
