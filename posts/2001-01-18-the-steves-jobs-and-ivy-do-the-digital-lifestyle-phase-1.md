title: The Steves (Jobs and Ivy) do the Digital Lifestyle, Phase 1
---
At Macworld SF, Steve Jobs layed out [Apple](htp://www.apple.com/)'s vision for itself and the future of the Macintosh. In a 15 minute section of his [keynote](), he described the [digital lifestyle](http://www.apple.com/enews/2001/01/11enews1.html#article1), where our Macs become "Digital Hubs" that route information and media between the new generation of digital devices that are becoming more and more commonplace.
