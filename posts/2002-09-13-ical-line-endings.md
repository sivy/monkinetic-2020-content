title: iCal line endings
---
It has been determined (by me) that [iCal](http://www.apple.com/ical/) borks on mac-style line endings (\r - oh, the irony!) [Morbus](http://www.disobey.com/dnn/) whipped up a perl cgi that will convert your mac-style line endings to unix ones, so you can subscribe to a mac-encoded calendar.
