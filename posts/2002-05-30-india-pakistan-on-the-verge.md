While the rest of the world hunts [terrorists](http://www.pbs.org/wgbh/pages/frontline/shows/binladen/who/alqaeda.html) or [monopolists](http://www.microsoft.com), India and Pakistan have had it up to their earlobes with each other and are amassing, well, [masses of soldiers](http://www.commondreams.org/headlines02/0524-01.htm) on the 2000 mile border between the two nations.
