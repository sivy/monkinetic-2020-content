title: Conversant Released To Developers
---
For the last few months, [Macrobyte](http://www.macrobyte.net/) has been working on readying [Conversant](http://conversant.macrobyte.net/), their internet groupware application, for release as a 'shrinkwrapped' product. Today marks the first release of [Conversant 1.0b1](http://conversant.macrobyte.net/news/2002/05/21#MSG229) to [Frontier](http://frontier.userland.com) and [Radio Userland](http://radio.userland.com) developers.
