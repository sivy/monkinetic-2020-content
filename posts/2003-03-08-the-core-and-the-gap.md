Thomas P.M. Barnett writes for the Navy War College on the [new understanding of the world](http://www.nwc.navy.mil/newrulesets/ThePentagonsNewMap.htm) from the Pentagon's viewpoint. It explains a lot of where our government sees the world going, and what our place in it will be.
>  Since the end of the cold war, the United States has been trying to come up with an operating theory of the world and a military strategy to accompany it.&nbsp; Now there's a leading contender.&nbsp; It involves identifying the problem parts of the world and aggressively shrinking them.
Agree or disagree, this is one to read.
