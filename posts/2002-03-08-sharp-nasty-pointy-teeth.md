title: Sharp, Nasty, Pointy teeth!
---
Working on Mac OS X for the last year and a half, I've come to the following conclusion, again: writers of unix utilities have an interesting idea of "usability" - "Fail Hard, Fail Fast, and Fail Painfully. That way the _User_ more quickly gains the _ability_ to do it right."
