title: Oh Yeah, Macs are expensive.
---
(Via "HBWT") Microsoft has changed its pricing on Windows licenses, going after the lucrative "what-you-do-with-it-after-we-sell-it-to-you" market. It effectively doubles the price of the OS for small to medium businesses, and oh, and it's going to make them [another __$11 bn__ in revenue](http://www.theregister.co.uk/content/1/13512.html).
