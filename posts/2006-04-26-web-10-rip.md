This morning a coworker was commenting on how cool it was that [Dojo](http://dojotoolkit.org/) includes functionality that will compress their JavaScript library by converting all human-readable javascript to short identifiers made up of underscores and numbers or letters ("_a","_1", etc). Compression births [obfuscation](http://www.answers.com/obfuscation).
