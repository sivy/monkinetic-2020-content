Last night Jodi and I went out to dinner at [Buca Di Beppo](http://bucadibeppo.com/), an incredibly popular italian restaurant in the area. Buca is famous for their "family-style" southern italian cuisine (read: "massive portions"), and 99% of their menu is made fresh daily.
