While chatting with our in-house designer (and Baron Banner) we got onto the topic of our respective design backgrounds and consequently our ability to identify fonts in various ads, etc.

<img align="right" border="0" src="https://media.redmonk.net/images/fontfont.jpg"/>

This got me thinking about my old habit of trawling through the old type foundries looking for cool fonts. My favs have always been from [Emigre](http://www.emigre.com/) and [FontFont](http://www.fontfont.com/). FontFont sells the [font](http://www.fontfont.de/packages/meta11025/meta11025.html) that the German gov't adopted for their highway signage back in '96 or so...
