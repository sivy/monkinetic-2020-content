Christian Pekeler of [Codefab](http://www.codefab.com/services.html) has put together a [radical little command line tool](http://www.pekeler.org/software/id2id/) called id2id for "Mac OS X". It takes a set of Obj-C interface definitions and outputs a graphical view of them, in OmniGraffle format.
