title: Sedona, May 2002
---
My parents, Jodi, and I went north to Sedona yesterday. We hiked up to [Cathedral Rock](http://media.redmonk.net/collections/sedona/Pages/Image10.html), a breathtakingly beautiful formation with a view for miles and miles. It was about a .7 mile hike, with a rise of about 600 feet.
